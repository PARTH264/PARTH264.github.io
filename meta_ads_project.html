Analysis

Key Insights & Strategic Findings

📘
Facebook Dominates Volume
Facebook delivers 216K impressions vs Instagram's 123.8K — 74.5% more reach on equal budgets ($2.5M each). Facebook wins on all absolute volume metrics: clicks, conversions, engagements and comments.
📸
Instagram More Efficient Per Impression
Despite lower volume, Instagram's CTR (11.86%) and ER (13.60%) edge ahead of Facebook. This suggests Instagram's smaller, more curated audience engages more deeply — quality over quantity.
🎯
Stories = Best Format on Facebook
Facebook Stories dominates with 72K impressions, 8K clicks, best CR 5.5% and PR 0.65%. It's the single best-performing ad format across the entire Meta portfolio — should receive highest budget allocation.
🖼️
Image Ads Win on Instagram
Instagram Image ads achieve 12.2% CTR (highest across all formats) and 13.9% ER. Instagram's visual-first audience responds best to high-quality single images over video or Stories formats.
📹
Video Underperforms on Instagram
Instagram Video recorded only 10K impressions and 1K clicks — the lowest of all formats. Facebook Video performs significantly better (46K impressions). Budget should be reallocated from IG Video to IG Image or Carousel.
👩
Female Audience Drives More Engagement
Females dominate engagement on both platforms — 43% on Facebook and 37% on Instagram. Creative targeting towards female demographics (especially 18-28) should be prioritized for maximum ROI.
