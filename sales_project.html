Analysis

Key Insights & Strategic Findings

🏆
West is the Star Region of 2024
West leads in every single metric — $250.1K sales (+33%), $43.8K profit (+82%) and 4,270 units (+41%). California and Washington are clearly the primary revenue drivers, confirmed by the state-level map distribution.
⚠️
Central Profit Collapsed 62%
Central's sales are flat at $147.1K (▼0%) but profit crashed from $19.9K to just $7.6K (▼62%). Selling more at lower margins — urgent review of Central region pricing, discounting practices and cost structure is critical.
📉
South Growing Revenue, Losing Profit
South grew sales impressively at ▲31% but profit halved from $17.7K to $8.8K (▼50%). This divergence between top-line and bottom-line growth signals margin erosion — likely from over-discounting or rising operational costs.
🌍
California is the #1 State
California tops the state map by a wide margin with the longest bar — ahead of New York (2nd) and Washington (3rd). The West region's strong performance is disproportionately driven by California's volume and revenue contribution.
📦
Quantity Up 27% — Margins Pressured
Total quantity grew ▲27% (9,870 → 12,515 units) while total profit grew only ▲14%. Units growing nearly 2× faster than profit means average selling price or margins are being sacrificed for volume — a potential long-term risk.
📅
Strong H2 Trend Across All Regions
All four region monthly sparklines show accelerating sales in Q4 (Nov–Dec), with South and West peaking in November. This seasonality pattern should inform 2025 inventory planning and sales target setting for H1 vs H2.
